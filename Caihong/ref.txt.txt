M851 Z0
Home all axes
G28

M211 S0
Move the nozzle down so it is just gripping a piece of standard printer paper
Set the Z-offset to the displayed value. E.g. if the printer displays a Z-Value of -1.23 enter
M851 Z-1.23
Store it to the EEPROM
M500
